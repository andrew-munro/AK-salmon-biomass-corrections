# AK-salmon-biomass-corrections
Corrected estimates of adult return biomass and total biomass for Alaska pink, chum, and sockeye salmon (1985-2015) from Ruggerone and Irvine (2018): Numbers and Biomass of Natural- and Hatchery-Origin Pink Salmon, Chum Salmon, and Sockeye Salmon in the North Pacific Ocean, 1925–2015.
